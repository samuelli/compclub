Started by the students of UNSW Computing, the High School Computing Club...
   

Started by the students of UNSW Computing, the High School Computing Club is an initiative established to help students explore the depth and breath of Computer Science.

From competitions to computer games, the Computing Club hopes to spark the interest of students who are just starting their journey with Computing while also providing depth for students that have excelled beyond what is taught in the class room.

We believe anyone can learn how to code, and that everyone should at least give it a go. Our mentors have come from varying Computing backgrounds, with some having learnt it at high school and others only picking it up at university.

Computer Science is a broad topic with fields in Robotics, Artificial intelligence, Networks, Security, Computer Graphics, Game design and many more. We hope to show students from all disciplines how a knowledge of Computing can help them in the future.
