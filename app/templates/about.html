About

The motivation for running High School Computing Club came when a bunch of UNSW's Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) students presented workshops and classes at the UNSW Computing 2012 Winter School. These students noticed that a lot of high school students are enthusiastic about computing, but there was inadaquate support available when they tried to learn on their own.

The High School Computing Club aims to bring together students with similar interests, where the CSE students are there to provide guidance and high school students support each other in developing their skills and expanding their knowledge.

In addition to this, the High School Computing Club runs workshops and events aimed specifically at giving high school students greater exposure to the more interesting aspects of computing that are not always taught in high schools computing classes, such as building a website, learning the basics of programming, and writing an artificial intelligent agent.
